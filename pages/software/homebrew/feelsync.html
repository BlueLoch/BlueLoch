Getting Started

FeelSync is available on both Android™ and iOS devices from the Google Play and App Store respectively. Simply type "FeelSync" into the searchbar and install it!

When you first open the app there will be a quick tutorial to show you where you can find the most important features.

FAQs


Why make FeelSync?

FeelSync started as a passion project with a single vision in mind: a completely free mental health app. No subscriptions, no features locked behind paywalls, no ads.

How much does FeelSync cost?

All core features of FeelSync will be completely free forever. We have plans to introduce themes to the app which will cost a small amount of money to help us keep FeelSync up and running.

Is FeelSync finished?

Nope! We plan to continue to develop FeelSync, adding new features and fixing bugs, for the forseeable future. If there's something you'd like to see, let us know at support@blueloch.co.uk!

How does FeelSync handle my data?

FeelSync stores all data you create on your device giving you complete control. For more information, see FeelSync's privacy policy.
