About

FeelSync is an app designed to help you understand your mental health. It let's you easily track your mood, thoughts and feelings, from the convenience of your phone, completely free. If you need a time-out, FeelSync has a number of exercises to help you get back in control.

FAQs


  • What are Syncs?

    Syncs are a record, created by you, of what you are thinking or feeling at a given time. Making Syncs allows you to track your mood throughout the day, as well as notice patterns in your emotions and thoughts.

  • Future features

    FeelSync isn't finished yet! We plan to continue to develop FeelSync adding new features such as more analytics, new and improved exercises, personalisation, and more! If you have something you'd like to see, or wish to report an issue, get in touch with us at contact@blueloch.co.uk!

How Tos


  • Make a Feeling Sync

    You can make any type of Sync from the home screen. To make a Feeling Sync:

    1. Tap any of the five emoji buttons under "Sync a feeling"
    2. If needed, select a more specific emotion from the dropdown
    3. Optionally, add what made you feel that way in the textbox
    4. Rate your mood from 1 (bad) to 5 (good)
    5. Click "Save"

    Note that if you click the grey "Neutral" feeling button, you won't have a dropdown to let you pick a more specific emotion.

  • Make a Thought Sync

    You can make any type of Sync from the home screen. To make a Thought Sync:

    1. Tap the light blue button under "Sync a thought"
    2. Type what the thought was in the textbox
    3. Optionally, select how the thought made you feel from the dropdown box
    4. Rate your mood from 1 (bad) to 5 (good)
    5. Click "Save"

  • Complete an exercise

    Exercises will take you through a number of steps to help you relax, focus on a thought or feeling, or bring yourself back to reality. To complete an exercise:

    1. In the main menu (≡), click "Exercises"
    2. If you wish to know more about an exercise, click the blue "Info" button
    3. Click the green "Start" button to begin the exercise
    4. If you see a page with some text, read through the text and click "Next"
    5. If you see a page with a textbox, fill in the textbox with the relevant information and click "Next"
    6. If you see a page with a pulsating blue circle, relax and breathe. Use the circle as a guide, but do not feel pressured to time your breathing with it. Once the timer reaches zero, the exercise will automatically move on
    7. Once you have completed all steps, you can click the green "Close" button to return to the "Exercises" screen

  • Review Syncs and exercises

    Once you have made a Sync, or completed an exercise, you can view that Sync and any others. To Review syncs and exercises:

    1. In the main menu (≡), click "Review"
    2. If you wish to review a day other than today, use the left and right arrows beside the date to choose the day
    3. If you wish to get to a long-gone day quickly, click the "Toggle Calendar" button and select the day on the calendar

  • Find crisis resources

    If you are struggling, FeelSync has a database of crisis helplines you can contact. To find these:

    1. In the main menu (≡), click "Crisis"
    2. Select your country from the dropdown menu
    3. A list of crisis resources for your country will now be visible

    Please note some countries have been excluded from the list as they have no dedicated crisis helplines, or because the app is not available in those countries.
    Additionally, some countries may not have an exhaustive list of helplines, such as the United States, as there are simply too many available.
    If you can't find help through FeelSync, you can try this link: Therapy Route.

  • Add a Survival Kit item

    You can add items to your Survival Kit to help you through tough times. To do this:

    1. In the main menu (≡), click "Crisis"
    2. Select the tab marked with a starred folder icon
    3. Click the green "New Item" button
    4. Fill in the information including the name, type of item, and the item's content. Text items will be displayed as they are, quote items displayed in quotes, and link items as clickable links
    5. Click the green "Save" button

  • Edit Survival Kit items

    Once you have made a Survival Kit item, you can edit it easily. To edit it:

    1. In the main menu (≡), click "Crisis"
    2. Select the tab marked with a starred folder icon
    3. Next to the item you want to edit, click the yellow button with a pencil icon
    4. Change what you need to change, then click the green "Save" button

  • Delete Survival Kit items

    Once you have made a Survival Kit item, you can delete it easily. To delete it:

    1. In the main menu (≡), click "Crisis"
    2. Select the tab marked with a starred folder icon
    3. Next to the item you want to edit, click the red button with a bin icon
    4. Click the "Delete" button to confirm you wish to delete the item

  • Delete your data

    If you wish to delete your data, you can do so easily from within the app. Note that this will only delete Syncs and exercises. You can delete Survival Kit items from the Survival Kit page. To delete data:

    1. In the main menu (≡), click "Settings"
    2. Click the red "Delete Data" button
    3. Select whether you wish to delete data over days, weeks or months
    4. Select how many days weeks or months you wish to delete data from
    5. Optionally, if you wish to delete all of your data, you can tick the "Delete all data" checkbox
    6. Click the red "Delete" button
    7. Click the "Delete" button to confirm you wish to delete the data
