Mobile App

About

The RedAgile mobile app, developed by BlueLoch Software Solutions, is designed for use alongside RedAgile's wide range of Scrum Alliance® certified courses. The app provides a virtual set of cards to play the Scrum Poker game, for which you can find instructions on how to play on the RedAgile website.

How to Use

The RedAgile app is available for free from both the Google Play store and Apple App Store. Android™ devices must be running Android™ version 11 or higher. Apple iOS and iPadOS® devices must be running version 11 or higher.

When you first open the app you will see a screen with a number of cards with numbers. Clicking each card will show a pop-up containing the number. The pop-up can be dismissed by tapping anywhere on the screen.

If you wish to see more information about RedAgile or the Planning Poker game, you can click the white "RedAgile" button in the top right of the screen to be taken to a screen with links to the RedAgile website, Planning Poker instructions, and contact details.
