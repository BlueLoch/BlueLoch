About

RedAgile

RedAgile is an award winning agile training and consulting business that host a wide variety of courses to expand the capabilities of project managers and software development teams across the globe. You can read more about RedAgile on their website.

The App

The RedAgile app aims to provide an all-in-one platform for attendees to participate in a wide range of activities during their course.

How to Use

Installation

The RedAgile mobile app is supported by most phone devices. This includes devices running Apple iOS 11 and higher, or Android 5.0 and higher. To install, follow the instructions for your device's operating system.

Android™

These instructions are for devices running Android™. This includes devices manufactured by Google, Samsung, Huawei, Sony and others.

  1. On your device, navigate to the Google Play store and open it.

  2. Search "RedAgile" in the Google Play store search bar.

  3. In the search results look for the app icon shown above and tap the listing.

  4. Click the blue "Install" button and wait for the app to finish downloading.

iOS / iPadOS®

These are instructions for devices running iOS or iPadOS®. These are devices manufactured by Apple.

  1. On your device, navigate to the App Store and open it.

  2. In the App Store, search for "RedAgile" and search.

  3. In the search results look for the app icon shown above.

  4. Click the blue download button to the right of the icon and wait for the app to finish downloading.

Using the App

When you open the app you will be presented with the Planning Poker exercise. To use this exercise, tap the numbers and a card will appear displaying the number in the centre of your screen.
