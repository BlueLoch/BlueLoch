General Troubleshooting

If you are having problems with our software, you can try one of the following. These will be most effective against performance issues, but may resolve other problems too.

  1. Restart the Software

    Sometimes when software has been running for a while it can clog up your device's resources. Closing the software and re-opening it can resolve this.

  2. Close Other Apps

    If your device is running into performance issues, try closing any programs running in the background. Programs running in the background can use up your device's resources slowing down the ones you are trying to use.

  3. Restart your Device

    As you use your device, it uses up its resources causing it to slow down over time. Restarting your device can help clear out its resources and get your software running smoothly again.

Closing Apps

Devices with Home Button

Double-press the home button and scroll along until you find the app listed. Swipe up on the preview to completely close it.

Devices without Home Button

On the home screen swipe up from the bottom of the screen and scroll along until you find the app listed. Swipe up on the preview to completely close it.
