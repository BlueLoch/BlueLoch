Privacy Policy

TL;DR

FeelSync stores all data it collects locally on your device. It collects any information you enter when creating Syncs or completing exercises, and generates some analytics for you to view in the "Review" page.

Types of Data

FeelSync only stores data that you provide. The following types of data are stored:


  • Mental Health data including your mood, emotions and thoughts
  • Date and time information related to when Syncs are made or exercises are completed
  • Sections of text, quotes or links from your Crisis Survival Kit

FeelSync uses this data to calculate useful statistics which can be viewed throughout the app. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • Average moods over varying time periods
  • Most commonly occurring emotions in a given day
  • Total numbers of Syncs and exercises on a given day

Calculated data is not stored and is created in circumstances where it is needed, such as when viewing information about a day.

Data Collection

FeelSync collects the majority of its data from what is provided by the user. This includes entries in text boxes and selections in dropdown menus. Additionally, FeelSync creates some data automatically when these actions are completed including the date and time at which a Sync was made or an exercise was complete.

Data Storage and Management

FeelSync stores all data locally on your device. This data is not stored on or shared to any external system controlled by BlueLoch Software Solutions or 3rd parties. Your device may automatically back-up this data if you have this feature enabled. Refer to the relevant privacy policy for your device to see how this data is handled.


Your data can be managed in "Settings > Data". From here you can delete data from however many days, weeks or months you wish to delete. You can also opt to delete all data. Note that any data deleted cannot be recovered. FeelSync will retain your data permanently unless deleted manually.

Enquiries and Requests

As FeelSync handles all data locally, we will be unable to fulfil any requests regarding the modification or deletion of your data.


If you have any questions or concerns regarding FeelSync's privacy policy, you can contact BlueLoch Software Solutions' data protection officer, Alastair Kilgour, at alastair@blueloch.co.uk.
