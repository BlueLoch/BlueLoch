Website Redesign

Hello!

Thank you for taking the time to help us test the redesign of our website. The idea of this testing program is to help us find any issues with the user experience, as well as the look and feel, of the redesign. When you click the "start" button below, a PDF will be downloaded to your device with complete instructions on how to carry out the testing. Please ensure you read and understand the instructions fully before you start testing.

Start Testing

What to expect

Once you have downloaded the PDF, you will need to keep it open to follow along with the instructions. It will ask you to open a survey prior to loading the website. Please only complete survey sections when asked to by the PDF! Once you have completed the first section of the survey, you will be asked to load up the new website in a seperate browser tab. Please follow the PDF's instructions on when to complete certain actions!

Privacy

The website redesign does not make use of any data collection or tracking technology. As such, it does not collect, use or sell your personal data.
When you load into the site, it will check your browser's theme settings to determine if you prefer light or dark mode. However, this data is discarded and the website will manage your preference internally for the remainder of the session.
In the same way, the website will store whether or not you have seen the cookie pop-up so it does not have to show it to you on every page. This data will be discarded at the end of your browser session.

The survey is hosted by Google Forms and is completely anonymous. Results will be stored in a secure location only accessible to BlueLoch Software Solutions. However, Google may collect some data. You can view Google's privacy policy here.
