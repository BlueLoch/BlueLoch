Welcome

We are a software development and consultancy firm based in Glasgow, Scotland. Our mission is to help you get the high-quality software you need for yourself or your business.

Whether you need a website, app, or some assistance setting up Microsoft 365, we can help.

Why choose us?

At BlueLoch, we promise three things: Quality, Inclusiveness and Innovation.

Quality

How many times have you heard of, or even witnessed, a piece of faulty software causing problems for a business? Your answer is probably 'too many'. We've also seen it too many times. That's why we promise a high-quality end product. All of our software is extensively tested before release. Plus, if we miss something, you can report it and we'll fix it at no extra cost.


Inclusiveness

In the not-so-distant past software companies would develop for clients using what was called the "waterfall approach". They would develop the complete product for the client before getting approval for it. Sure it worked sometimes, but what if the client wasn't happy with it? Projects would face major delays and run far overbudget.

At BlueLoch, we do things differently. We include you in the development process. From interface mock-ups to features, everything must get your seal of approval before it is integrated into the final product. If you aren't happy with something we'll go back and fix it. You'll receive regular updates on our progress, and demos of parts of the software as it is completed. That way, when it comes to the final product, you can be confident it is exactly what you need.


Innovation

Since the invention of the first computer, there have been a lot of ways of developing software. From wires plugged into boards, to today's modern frameworks and programming languages, the world of computing has come far. Yet many companies refuse to embrace the latest innovations.

At BlueLoch, we choose to use the latest and greatest frameworks to build your software. By doing so we can develop applications for every major platform faster. That includes Windows, MacOS®, Android™ and iOS. Plus, using modern frameworks ensures your software is supported for many years to come.
